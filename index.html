International Tourism in Malaysia

About this project

This project was an individual school assignment completed in September 2021.

The objective of this project was to design and create an effective and interactive visualisation that uses non-geographic idioms to present the information and data of a specific domain of our choice while applying the key principles of Data Visualisation.

Project outcomes

I chose the domain of my visualisation to be International Tourism in Malaysia, with the aim to provide an overview of how this industry performed and affected the economy and employment in Malaysia from 2010 to 2019.

My visualisation was based on the following 4 datasets:

  • ASEAN Statistical Yearbook 2020
  • Top 45 Tourist Arrivals
  • Tourism Satellite Accounts
  • Employment in the Tourism Industry 2000-2019

You can find my visualisation here , which was created using Tableau.
